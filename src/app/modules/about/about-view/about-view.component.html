Hi! My name is Ophélie, I’m in my 20’s and I live in Belgium. I am a very creative person and have been pretty much my whole life. I have followed a few courses at the Academy of Beaux-Arts when I was little but quickly realized that I much more enjoyed the freedom of an atelier.

So, whether it is painting or photography, I’m pretty much an autodidact (except for food photography, for which I took a course). I enjoy creating art that is very vibrant, with a lot of contrast and bright colors.

Because I’m a creative person, it is hard for me to stick to only one category when it comes to art.

So, regarding photography my main passion is portrait, but I also really enjoy photographing events and lately, food! I am that person that often carries a camera even when it’s not really necessary.

Painting, on the other hand, is a very therapeutic experience for me. It helps me clear my head and I don’t really choose in advance the outcome, I just trust the process.

Welcome into my universe, I hope you enjoy it!
